Julia is 25 years old and lives in Pittstown, NJ with her boyfriend, two dogs, and cat. She has a BS in Ecology, Evolution, and Natural Resources from Rutgers University.

Julia started Rutgers Coding Bootcamp in June 2018 and is pursuing a career in programming. She got her start in web development while working at an environmental nonprofit at her hometown in Pennington, NJ. Her organization tasked her with creating a website, and her curiosity was sparked once she got her feet wet in web development.

Julia loves the outdoors and spends as much time outside as possible, when she's not inside coding! She is an avid runner and has run two half marathons so far, and is training for a full marathon in September. She also enjoys cooking, baking, and drinking kombucha.
